# Nexovia Governance & Compliance

## 1. Mission and Nature
Nexovia operates as a **Technical Publication** and research laboratory. The primary objective is the dissemination of technical knowledge and the collaborative development of open-source standards.

## 2. Compliance & Non-Commercial Status

* **Non-Employment:** Nexovia is not a business entity, employer, or service provider. Participation is voluntary and collaborative.
* **No Commercial Services:** We do not offer paid consulting, enterprise support, or proprietary software licenses.
* **Infrastructure Support:** Any financial support received via GitHub Sponsors is directed toward the **Nexovia Project Fund**. These funds are used solely for project-related expenses such as domain hosting, CI/CD runners, cloud computing cost and lab hardware.

## 3. Leadership Structure
The project is guided by the **Lead Maintainer**. Leadership transitions and maintainer roles are granted based on consistent technical merit and adherence to the **IFOS** framework.

## 4. Licensing
All research, specifications, and code within the Nexovia organization are released under the **Apache License 2.0** or **MIT License**.
